about us

Architecture is the mirror of our dreams

Nitty Gritty Architecture was founded in 2013, committing to making cities, towns, individual homes, schools, malls better places to live

We believe architecture and design produce significant change in people's lives therefore, we strive to create buildings that best interact with people that will use them'

Our architecture firm boasts more than 1000 clients with partners including First National Bank, Standard Bank and Kabu Designs just to mention a few

We embrace a responsibility to make our projects a success therefore we take your project from the beggining, municipal hassles until plan approval.
